COVID Infometer is a comprehensive tool designed to provide up-to-date information on COVID-19 cases, statistics, and trends both in Nepal and around the world. With COVID Infometer, you can easily access reliable and real-time information to make informed decisions and stay updated on the situation.

Nepal COVID-19 Data
Get the latest COVID-19 statistics specifically for Nepal. Stay informed about the total number of cases, active cases, recoveries, and unfortunate fatalities.
Global COVID-19 Data
Explore the worldwide impact of the pandemic with data from various countries across the globe.
Interactive Dashboard
Easily navigate through different sections, switch between Nepal and global data, and visualize trends through charts and graphs.
Real-time Updates
Get access to the most recent and accurate data by providing real-time updates. Stay informed about new cases, recoveries, and fatalities as they occur.
